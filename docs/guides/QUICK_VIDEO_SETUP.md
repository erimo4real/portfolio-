# 🚀 Quick Video Setup - 5 Minutes

## The Fastest Way to Add Videos to Your Blog

### Step 1: Upload to YouTube (2 minutes)
1. Go to https://studio.youtube.com
2. Click "Create" → "Upload videos"
3. Upload your video
4. Add title and description
5. Click "Publish"

### Step 2: Get Embed Code (30 seconds)
1. Go to your video
2. Click "Share" button
3. Click "Embed"
4. Copy the `<iframe>` code

### Step 3: Add to Blog Post (1 minute)
1. Go to your admin panel: http://localhost:5173/admin/blogs
2. Create or edit a blog post
3. In the markdown field, paste:

```markdown
# Your Blog Title

Here's my video tutorial:

<iframe width="100%" height="400" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/YOUR_VIDEO_ID" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Your written content here...
```

4. Click "Save" or "Publish"

### Step 4: View Your Post (30 seconds)
1. Go to http://localhost:5173/blog
2. Click on your post
3. Video is now embedded! 🎉
